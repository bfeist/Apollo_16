[In January 2011, Ulli Lotzmann realized that photos AS16-117-18842 and 18843 show a piece of Beta Cloth on which Charlie has written "64-C" to honor the third class of 1964 at the USAF Aerospace Research Pilot School. In Moonwalker Charlie mentions three of the others: Sam Armstrong, who is standing on Charlie's left in the picture; Joe Wuertz, who is on Charlie's right; and Pete Hoag, who is on Al Worden's right. I also found a bio of "Duke" Fredricks, who is on Wuertz's right at the left end of the line; and Jerry Larson, who is kneeling in front of Charlie. In a February 2011 exchange of e-mail, Charlie identified the pilot kneeling on the right as Gary Moore, but said that the class had "lost track of him". Two of the signatures remain uncertain: Floyd Stoorep(?), kneeling on the left; and "Windy" Hull, kneeling, second from the left.]
