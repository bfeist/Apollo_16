[A vesicular rock is one that contains the imprints of gas bubbles trapped in the melt from which the rock solidified. Vesicular basalts were found at the Apollo 11, 12, 15, and 17 sites and vesicular breccias were found at the Apollo 17 site. Apollo 17 photo AS17-141-21628 shows large vesicules in the Station 6 boulder.]
